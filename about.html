About


How it works, who we are, why we're here

FutureTee is a company that specializes in customizable clothing. We're a little different, though. We're procedurally influenced by technology and the intelligent digital friends we've been able to make in recent years.

Due to algorithms taking on a certain level of complexity in their problem solving, we're able to efficiently produce incredible works and images inspired by some of humanity's most genius artists.

Based on the algorithms from a reknowned paper called "A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style", our system was engineered with an advanced version of this algorithm implemented.

This algorithm is quite good at replicating art styles and we've coded a platform, for anyone to use, that allows them to replicate any style in their own photos.

1. Upload your image

The first step is to upload any jpg, gif, or png image

2. Powerful Algorithms Process

Advanced machine learning technology will apply the art style you chose to your image.

3. Wait

There is approximately a 10 second wait for the processing to finish.

4. Finished!

Once the server is done with your request, the page will refresh and you can now see your work of art!

5. Save

Feel free to come back and just use your email to see your past works of art.

6. Buy

We offer a huge selection of prints and accessories with your generated art on it.
